OSPC

The onsite Programming Contest is the offline Algorithm contest between the participants. They are not only tested their Algorithm adrenaline , also the grip over various other general knowledge on computer science.
TEAM :A team consists a maximum of three participants, not necessarily from same college or department.

FORMAT

ROUND 1

It tests the grip over various domains of computer science. It is a paper-pen based test, and acts as an eliminating round. Out of all the teams, only top 8 teams move forward to the next round.Time limit-60 minutes

ROUND 2

It consist of questions, asking to write codes to solve them. The entire team will be allocated one computer and they will have to solve the maximum number of questions. The questions will be evaluated on the various test cases. Time Limit: 90 minutes

PRIZE:

FIRST: 5000

SECOND: 3000

THIRD: 2000
