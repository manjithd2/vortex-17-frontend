Registration & Accommodation

Accommodation is also provided on spot on first come first serve basis but it is advised that you pre-book your accomodation as there might be shortage of accomodation facilities.
It is mandatory to bring your college ID card and a print out or soft copy of your VORTEX ID (which can be obtained once you register and login into the website) when you arrive at NIT, Trichy.
Accommodation will be provided in a common room on sharing basis. However, large teams or teams from the same college may be put up in the same rooms.

  • PR Registration Charge : Rs.150/- per person.
  • Accommodation Charge : Rs.150/- per day per person.
  • Caution deposit : Rs.100/- per person.

PR Registration is mandatory for all participants and you will be provided with welcome kits. Participant will be charged on 24-hour basis, that is you will be charged from the time of check-in till the time you check out. If any delay is made in checking out, caution deposit will not be refunded. Accommodation will start only from 16:00, on 3rd of February, and participants should vacate the rooms before 23:59 on 5th of February. Please plan your travel accordingly. Female participants are requested to reach the campus before 9:00 pm on the day of arrival.

REACH US
Trichy is connected by road to Chennai and to all major cities and towns in South India by regular bus services. Tiruchirapalli is an important junction on the Southern Railway. It connects Chennai, Thanjavur, Madurai, Tuticorin, Tirupati, Rameswaram, Bangalore,Coimbatore, Cochin and Mangalore. The simplest and most economical way to reach NIT-Trichy is by bus. Any city Bus at Tiruchirapalli Junction will take you to the central bus stand or Chattram bus stand. From Central Bus Stand: Board Thanjavur bound mofussil bus or a route bus(number: 128 bounded towards Thuvakudi). The journey time from Trichy will be around 40 minutes. From Chattram Bus Stand: Many buses depart from Chattram bus stand. Bus number 26 will be bounded towards NIT-T). If direct buses are not available, buses towards Thiruverumbur may be available.
